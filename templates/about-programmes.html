About Programmes

In-House Courses

An In – house/ tailor made course is presented exclusively to delegates from your company. This allows for a large degree of customization.

Fee
If more than 10 people attend, a substantial discount kicks in.
Location
You may prefer to move your staff from all work-related distractions which have a negative effect on their training. The course will run at a location that suits you (hotel/conference centre).
Date & Time
The course will run on a date that suits your companies’ and delegates’ schedules allowing for the least interference with work. Time will be arranged to suit your needs.
Content
The course content will be structured around your delegates’ needs. The case studies, examples and role plays will be relative to your company, market, delegates’ positions and departments.

More Information

Venue

All 3 weeks scheduled programmes are conducted in Manzini Swaziland. 1 week scheduled programmes can also be conducted in Windhoek Namibia and Durban South Africa and 2 weeks scheduled programmes can also be conducted in Durban South Africa. Please note: The Institute has the right to cancel programmes to be conducted outside Manzini Swaziland if the number of participants is less than ten (10). 4 weeks courses are conducted on request. Contact us for more information.

Applications

Our courses are open to all individuals who qualify for nomination and funding from their employers and other sponsors or self sponsorship. Participants/ Organisations may apply online or contact our office for registration or further information about the programmes. All courses are conducted in English and course profiles are provided on request by the prospective applicant(s).
